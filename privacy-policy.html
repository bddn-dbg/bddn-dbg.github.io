🔐 Introduction & Scope

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Bihar Digital Data and Network ("BDDN", "we", "us", or "our"), operating the website bddn.online, collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information provided by visitors and clients ("you" or "User") when you access or use our website and services.

BDDN is a professional digital agency headquartered in Darbhanga, Bihar, India, offering website development, SEO optimization, logo design, e-commerce development, and social media integration services.

This Policy is prepared in compliance with:

  • ✓The Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India
  • ✓The Information Technology Act, 2000 and its amendments
  • ✓The Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011
  • ✓General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles, applied on a best-efforts basis

By accessing our website at https://bddn.online and submitting any form or contacting us, you acknowledge and consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

04

🍪 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

4.1 Current Cookie Usage

Based on analysis of our codebase, BDDN's website does not implement any proprietary cookies or client-side tracking scripts. No cookie consent banner is currently deployed because no analytics or advertising cookies are set by BDDN directly.

4.2 Third-Party Cookies & Browser Storage

Third-party services integrated into our website may set their own cookies or access browser storage:

  • ✓Google Fonts: Requests load from Google CDN, logging browser IP. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • ✓WhatsApp: Clicking chat launches Meta platforms which store tracking cookies.
  • ✓Google Apps Script: Form transmissions to Google web endpoints process browser metadata.

4.3 Local Storage / Session Storage

Our JavaScript scripts do not write persistent data in localStorage or sessionStorage.

4.4 How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking third-party cookies is recommended if you wish to limit network logging, though it may affect font displays.

05

🔗 Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with or links to the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy and data practices:

5.1 Google Apps Script & Google Sheets

Enquiry form data is transmitted to a Google Apps Script Web App (deployed at script.google.com) which writes the data to a private Google Sheets spreadsheet accessible only to BDDN. The data fields transmitted include: Name, Mobile Number, Email, Business Type, Service Needed, Budget Range, Message, and Timestamp. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5.2 Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts CDN to load the Rajdhani and Nunito typefaces. When your browser loads these fonts, a request is made to Google's servers, which may log your IP address.

5.3 WhatsApp (Meta Platforms)

Our website provides direct WhatsApp links (wa.me/917982229379) for customer communication. Once you open WhatsApp, your interactions are governed by WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.

5.4 GitHub Pages (Hosting)

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages (github.io infrastructure). GitHub may collect server-level logs including IP addresses of visitors. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

5.5 BDDN Institute (External Link)

Our website contains a link to "BDDN Institute" (an associated educational platform). Clicking this link will take you to an external website with its own separate privacy policy.

⚠️
Not Present: The following tracking integrations are not active in the codebase: Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Google Ads tags, payment gateways, live chats, or marketing SDKs.

⚖️ Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India and applicable data protection principles, you have the following rights regarding your personal data held by BDDN:

  • ✓Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • ✓Right to Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • ✓Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal overrides.
  • ✓Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw your consent at any time.
  • ✓Right to Grievance Redressal: You may raise complaints directly with our Grievance Officer.
  • ✓Right to Nominate: You may nominate another individual to manage your rights under DPDP guidelines.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@bddn.online or via WhatsApp at +91 79822 29379. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

📬
If you are a resident of the European Union or European Economic Area and believe your GDPR rights apply, you may also contact your local Data Protection Authority. However, BDDN primarily operates under Indian jurisdiction.
09

🛡️ Security Measures

BDDN takes the security of your personal data seriously and implements the following measures:

  • ✓HTTPS Encryption: Encrypted data-in-transit via TLS.
  • ✓No-CORS Fetch: Requests sent with limited headers to minimize cross-origin data exposure.
  • ✓Access Controls: Database restricted to authorized personnel via Google authentication.
  • ✓Static Architecture: Simple HTML page delivery without dynamic databases, reducing the attack surface.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach, BDDN will issue notices in accordance with the IT Act and DPDP guidelines.

10

👶 Children's Privacy

BDDN's services are not directed to children under the age of 18 years. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. Our enquiry forms are intended for business owners, entrepreneurs, and adult professionals seeking digital services.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has submitted personal information to us without your consent, please contact us immediately at info@bddn.online and we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, processing personal data of children requires verifiable parental consent. We do not seek to process any child's personal data.
