About Bihar Digital Data & Network

Bihar Digital Data and Network (BDDN) is a professional digital agency based in Darbhanga, Bihar. Our mission is simple: Learn Digital, Build Future.

We empower local businesses, schools, clinics, restaurants, and entrepreneurs with affordable, professional, and high-quality digital solutions — helping them establish and grow their online presence. We understand the unique needs of Bihar businesses and craft solutions that work for them.

Founded in 2024, BDDN has quickly become one of Darbhanga's most trusted names in digital services. From a basic landing page to a full e-commerce store, we deliver quality results on time and within budget.
