SEO FAQs

SEO Services FAQ – Darbhanga

Answers to common questions about SEO for Bihar businesses.

What is local SEO and why does my Darbhanga business need it?

Local SEO helps your business appear in Google when people in Darbhanga or Bihar search for your services. For example, "doctor in Darbhanga" or "restaurant near me". Local SEO ensures your business shows up in these results.

How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. Most businesses start seeing improvements within 2–3 months. Significant and sustained results typically appear in 4–6 months. The results last much longer than paid advertising.

Do I need SEO if I already have a website?

Yes! Having a website is just the first step. Without SEO, your website may not appear in Google search results at all. SEO makes your website discoverable to potential customers actively searching for your services.

What is Google Business Profile?

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is a free tool that lets you manage how your business appears on Google Search and Maps. It's essential for local businesses in Darbhanga to attract nearby customers.
